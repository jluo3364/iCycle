Frequently Asked Questions

Fast Fashion stores like Zara and Shein are increasing our carbon footprint aroudn the world. We created this app in order to allow others to resell their clothing and promote thrifting in order to help recycle clothes.

Most thrifting apps like Depop or Poshmark have higher prices and have unofficial trading. We decided to choose the prices based on the conditions of the clothing and inform the seller and with their aproval, place the item on sale in our app. Therfore we do not have any unoffical trading or higher prices, so we can ensure valid prices for items.

If we had more time, we would try to figure out a way to send products on the website or on the app. We would also try to figure out a way to import the answers for the google form to the app.
